CRITICAL: Run Both Directions Equally!

Why? Beaches slope toward the water. Running in one direction creates uneven stress on your hips and lower back.

Solution: Run OUT for 10-15 minutes, then BACK for 10-15 minutes. This balances the camber.

Check Tide Times

  • Best: Low tide = more packed sand available
  • Avoid: High tide = less space, softer surface
  • Tip: 5:15am Tuesday runs - check if tide is favorable
